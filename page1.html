Ainish Philip
As leader of Anointing Fire Catholic Youth Ministry Ainish is a full- time evangelist who devotes all her time working tirelessly towards the salvation of souls. On a weekly basis, she activley travels around the United States, as well as internationally, leading life altering retreats for youth. As Ainish constantly strives to plant seeds of faith into the hearts of youth worldwide, she trains hundreds of youth to give their lives to Jesus and instill the Word of God in their hearts through evangelization.

"The Witnesses"
The Witnesses is the musical band of Anointing Fire Catholic Youth Ministry. Through this band, we plan to reach out to as many souls of the youth in North America through Praise and Worship. Each band member is working earnestly to spread the joy which they have received while they greeted Christ Jesus their own.
